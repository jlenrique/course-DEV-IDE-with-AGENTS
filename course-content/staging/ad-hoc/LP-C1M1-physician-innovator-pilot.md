# Lesson Plan — Physician as Innovator (AV Pilot)

## Header

- **Module:** C1 — Foundations of the Innovation Mindset (M1)
- **Lesson:** PILOT-PHYS-INN — *The Physician as Innovator* (narrated slide deck — ad-hoc)
- **Learning Objectives:** (from SME Module 1 CLOs; this pilot emphasizes 1, 3, 5; supports 2 & 4 as preview hooks)
  1. **CLO1** (Understand/Apply): Define the *innovation mindset* and correlate core clinical competencies (e.g. diagnostic reasoning, empathy) with innovation frameworks such as Design Thinking.
  2. **CLO2** (Analyze): Analyze macro-economic and structural trends that necessitate intrapreneurial physician leadership. *(Pilot: light touch via “case for change” framing on one slide — full depth deferred.)*
  3. **CLO3** (Analyze/Evaluate): Differentiate a superficial *idea* from a vetted *opportunity* (Intrapreneurship Formula / Lam).
  4. **CLO4** (Evaluate): Evaluate systemic operational failures via root-cause thinking and peer forum (“M&M of Innovation”). *(Pilot: referenced as mindset only — full activity deferred.)*
  5. **CLO5** (Create): Create a personal innovation baseline (SWOT) and Opportunity Radar. *(Pilot: closing call-to-action; full artifacts deferred.)*
- **Estimated Duration:** ~12–18 minutes narration (10 slides; pacing at Gate 2)
- **Prerequisites:** None (module opener / pilot slice)
- **Pairing Reference:** LP-C1M1-PILOT-PHYSINN
- **Source bundle:** `course-content/staging/ad-hoc/source-bundles/pilot-physician-innovator/extracted.md`
- **Run mode:** ad-hoc (scratch staging only)

## Instructional strategy

Single narrative arc: **legitimize the physician as innovator** → **show transferable clinical cognition** → **position intrapreneurship and opportunity discipline** as the operational path inside health systems. Cognitive load: one main framework comparison slide (Diagnosis ↔ Design Thinking); avoid stacking multiple new models on one card.
