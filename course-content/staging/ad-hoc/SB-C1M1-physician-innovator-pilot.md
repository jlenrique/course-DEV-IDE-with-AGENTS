### Slide 6

**Content:**
- **Headline:** Physicianship **and** innovation leadership
- **Venn or overlap center:** Empathy · Curiosity · Analytical prowess · Resiliency · Comfort with ambiguity
- **Takeaway:** Apply existing clinical traits to **operational and system** problems.

**Visual Guidance:**
- Layout: Venn diagram or trait matrix

### Slide 7

**Content:**
- **Headline:** The bridge: **Clinical method ↔ Design Thinking**
- **Left column — Clinical method:** History → Exam/data → **Diagnosis** → Treatment plan
- **Right column — Design Thinking:** Empathize → **Define** → Ideate → Prototype / test
- **On-slide bridges (arrows or labels):** History ↔ Empathize · Diagnosis ↔ Define · Treatment ↔ Ideate/Prototype
- **Bottom line:** Same cognitive loop — the “patient” becomes a **broken workflow or system**.

**Visual Guidance:**
- Layout: **two-column parallel** flowcharts (mandatory)
- Hero Element: parallel columns + connecting lines
- Visual Density: balanced (labels short; no paragraph text)
- Image Guidance: blue (clinical) vs green (design) pathway optional

**Learning Purpose:** CLO1 **threshold concept** — transferable expertise.

**Behavioral / Affective Intent:** “Aha” — reframes daily work as innovation practice.

---

### Slide 8

**Content:**
- **Headline:** Intrapreneurship vs entrepreneurship
- **Intrapreneur:** Innovate **inside** the organization — constraints, politics, resources — scale via existing brand, capital, population
- **Entrepreneur:** Build **new** venture outside / separate
- **Punch line:** Fastest path to impact for many clinicians = **intrapreneurship**

**Visual Guidance:**
- Layout: split-screen definitions + icons
- Visual Density: minimal–balanced

**Learning Purpose:** Position where work happens; bridge to Lam / formula (reading in full module).

**Behavioral / Affective Intent:** Pragmatic, politically realistic.

---

### Slide 9

**Content:**
- **Headline:** Idea vs opportunity
- **Idea:** A thought — e.g. “We should use AI for notes” — **unvetted**
- **Opportunity:** Vetted for **desirability, feasibility, viability** in *your* organization — path to execution
- **Course promise:** Move from brainstorming ideas to **engineering opportunities**

**Visual Guidance:**
- Layout: two-column contrast or “bad vs good” simple graphic
- Visual Density: minimal

**Learning Purpose:** CLO3 — discriminating concept for downstream modules.

**Behavioral / Affective Intent:** Disciplined, serious — “we’re not here for slide filler ideas.”

---

### Slide 10

**Content:**
- **Headline:** What you leave this arc with
- **Bullets:**
  - **Gap:** Expert in care delivery ≠ automatic training as **system innovator**
  - **Mission:** Lead change **from within** using structured process + safety to speak up
  - **Next:** **Opportunity Radar** + personal **SWOT** (full module activities)
- **Closing:** The work ahead starts with how you **see** your clinical environment.

**Visual Guidance:**
- Layout: recap with forward arrow
- Visual Density: sparse
- Image Guidance: optional single forward/chevron motif

**Learning Purpose:** CLO5 forward pull; synthesize CLO1 + CLO3.

**Behavioral / Affective Intent:** Determined, clear next step.
