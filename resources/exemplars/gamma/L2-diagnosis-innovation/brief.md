# Exemplar: Diagnosis = Innovation

## Level: L2 (Simple-Moderate)

Single slide. Text-focused explanatory slide with a bold conceptual headline and supporting paragraph.

## What This Is

A single Gamma slide that makes a bold conceptual claim ("Diagnosis = Innovation") and supports it with a brief explanatory paragraph. Visually simpler than L1 — no columns or complex layout. The power is in the concise, direct framing.

## Content

**Title**: Diagnosis = Innovation

**Body**: Your clinical training has already prepared you to be an innovator. The process you use every day mirrors the innovation process exactly.

## Why It's Good

- The title is a provocative equation that challenges assumptions
- The body is exactly two sentences — no wasted words
- Bridges the familiar (clinical training) to the unfamiliar (innovation)
- Affirming tone — "you're already prepared" — builds confidence
- Clean, uncluttered single-focus slide

## Context Relationship

- Course: Applied Professional Communication / Innovation in Medicine
- Audience: Medical professionals who may not see themselves as innovators
- Learning objective: Students understand that their clinical diagnostic skills ARE innovation skills
- Pedagogical approach: Reframing — connecting existing identity to new capability

## What the Agent Should Learn

1. Not every slide needs complex layout — sometimes a bold title + short paragraph is most effective
2. Gamma can produce clean single-focus slides with minimal content
3. The challenge is getting Gamma to NOT over-generate — the agent needs to constrain output length
4. `textOptions.amount: brief` is critical for this style
5. `additionalInstructions` should emphasize "minimal text, bold statement, clean layout"

## Reproduction Notes

- `numCards: 1`, `textMode: preserve` (content is already final)
- `textOptions.amount: brief` to prevent over-generation
- `additionalInstructions`: request single-focus layout with bold headline
- Success = same provocative title, same concise body, no extra content added
