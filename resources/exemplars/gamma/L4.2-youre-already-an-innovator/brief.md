# Exemplar: You're Already an Innovator

## Level: L4.2 (Complex)

Single slide. Three-part progressive narrative with a storytelling arc (past → present → future).

## What This Is

A single Gamma slide that tells a three-beat story of empowerment: your training prepared you, your skills transfer, your impact awaits. Each beat has a heading and supporting sub-text. The structure is narrative, not informational — it builds momentum toward a call to action.

## Content

**Beat 1 — Your Training Prepared You:**
Clinical reasoning = Innovation thinking

**Beat 2 — Your Skills Transfer:**
Diagnosis, experimentation, iteration

**Beat 3 — Your Impact Awaits:**
Transform healthcare from within

## Why It's Good

- Three-beat narrative arc: foundation → evidence → call to action
- Progressive energy — each beat is more forward-looking than the last
- Concise sub-text that reinforces without repeating
- Functions as a summary/conclusion slide for the lesson
- Emotionally engaging while remaining professional

## Context Relationship

- Course: Applied Professional Communication / Innovation in Medicine
- Audience: Medical professionals at the end of a lesson module
- Learning objective: Students feel empowered to apply innovation thinking in their clinical practice
- Pedagogical approach: Motivational synthesis — wrapping up key themes with forward-looking energy

## What the Agent Should Learn

1. Gamma can produce narrative progression slides with three escalating beats
2. The visual layout should convey progression (left-to-right or top-to-bottom)
3. `additionalInstructions` should request "three-step progression" or "narrative arc layout"
4. Harder than L3's three-column because this isn't three equal items — it's a STORY with rising energy
5. The agent must understand the difference between parallel structure (L3) and progressive narrative (L4.2)
6. Sub-text should be minimal — supporting, not restating

## Reproduction Notes

- `numCards: 1`, `textMode: preserve`
- `additionalInstructions`: request progressive narrative layout with three escalating beats
- The three beats should feel like they build momentum, not sit side by side as equals
- Success = recognizable as a narrative arc; progression from past → present → future is visually clear; concise sub-text
