# Capability authoring (optional extensions)

Owners may teach Desmond **new** repeatable patterns — e.g., a named **export preset**, **folder convention**, or **QC macro** the team uses every run.

## Rules

- New capabilities must be **outcome-stated** (what good looks like), not 50-step UI click lists that will rot on the next Descript update.
- Link any procedural detail to **dated doc cache** or **owner-confirmed** habit in `BOND.md`.
- Register the capability in sanctum `CAPABILITIES.md` under **Learned** with one paragraph and the date.

## Anti-pattern

Pasting a full Descript feature tutorial into CAPABILITIES.md — instead, summarize the **decision** the team made and **where** to look in `references/cache/` for screenshots/terms.
