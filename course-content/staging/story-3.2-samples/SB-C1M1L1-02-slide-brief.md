### Slide 2 (SB-C1M1L1-02b): Flexibility & Resiliency

**Content:**
**Flexibility:** Willingness to change approach when evidence suggests a different path.
Clinical parallel: Adjusting treatment when labs are unexpected.

**Resiliency:** Capacity to recover from setbacks without losing momentum.
Clinical parallel: Learning from cases that didn't go as planned.

**Visual Guidance:**
- Layout: two-column-parallel
- Hero Element: Side-by-side comparison of clinical and innovation contexts
- Visual Density: balanced — two concepts, two clinical parallels
- Image Guidance: No image — text comparison is the visual

**Learning Purpose:**
Define the first two characteristics with immediate clinical grounding.

### Slide 3 (SB-C1M1L1-02c): Adaptability & Comfort with Ambiguity

**Content:**
**Adaptability:** Adjusting to new conditions in real time.
Clinical parallel: ED environments change hour by hour.

**Comfort with Ambiguity:** Operating with incomplete information.
Clinical parallel: Differential diagnosis = holding multiple hypotheses simultaneously.

**Visual Guidance:**
- Layout: two-column-parallel (mirrors slide 2 for consistency)
- Hero Element: The ambiguity definition — this is the hardest concept
- Visual Density: balanced
- Image Guidance: No image

**Learning Purpose:**
Complete the four-characteristic framework. Ambiguity is the key concept for this lesson.
